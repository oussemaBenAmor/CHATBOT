Payments Policy - Version 2

1. Purpose
This document outlines the policy for payments handled by our organization.

2. Policy Summary
We aim to ensure all payments are processed efficiently. Contact support@company.com for issues or clarifications.

3. Payment Processing
Credit card payments are verified instantly.
Verification ensures secure and immediate processing.

4. Payment Processing
Bank transfers incur a 1% processing fee for international accounts.
This fee covers additional banking costs for international transfers.

5. Payment Methods
Cheque payments require 5 business days to clear.
Clearance time ensures funds are available before shipping.

6. Payment Rules
Payment refunds are processed to the original method within 5 days.
Refunds are returned to the original payment source for consistency.
Disputes for payments must be filed within 10 days.
Extended reporting period allows flexibility for dispute resolution.

7. Summary
- Payments: Credit card payments are verified instantly. (Verification ensures secure and immediate processing.)
- Payments: Bank transfers incur a 1% processing fee for international accounts. (This fee covers additional banking costs for international transfers.)
- Payments: Cheque payments require 5 business days to clear. (Clearance time ensures funds are available before shipping.)
- Payments: Payment refunds are processed to the original method within 5 days. (Refunds are returned to the original payment source for consistency.)
- Payments: Disputes for payments must be filed within 10 days. (Extended reporting period allows flexibility for dispute resolution.)
