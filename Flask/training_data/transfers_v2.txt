Transfers Policy - Version 2

1. Purpose
This document outlines the policy for transfers handled by our organization.

2. Policy Summary
We aim to ensure all transfers are processed efficiently. Contact support@company.com for issues or clarifications.

3. Transfer Guidelines
Domestic transfers are completed within 2 business days.
Faster processing for domestic accounts enhances efficiency.

4. Transfer Guidelines
International transfers require a 3% fee and 5-day processing.
Higher fees and time account for international regulations.

5. Transfer Policies
Recipient details must be verified via email before transfer.
Email verification adds an extra layer of security.

6. Transfer Conditions
Transfers to unverified accounts are not permitted.
This policy prevents fraudulent transactions.
Disputes for transfers must be reported within 7 days.
Extended reporting period allows for thorough dispute resolution.

7. Summary
- Transfers: Domestic transfers are completed within 2 business days. (Faster processing for domestic accounts enhances efficiency.)
- Transfers: International transfers require a 3% fee and 5-day processing. (Higher fees and time account for international regulations.)
- Transfers: Recipient details must be verified via email before transfer. (Email verification adds an extra layer of security.)
- Transfers: Transfers to unverified accounts are not permitted. (This policy prevents fraudulent transactions.)
- Transfers: Disputes for transfers must be reported within 7 days. (Extended reporting period allows for thorough dispute resolution.)
